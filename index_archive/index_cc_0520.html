Hello!

I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering in 2026 at Aalto University, advised by Professors Esa Ollila and Sergiy A. Vorobyov. I received my M.Sc. in Acoustics from Aalto University in 2022, and my B.Eng. in Information Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, in 2020.

Research Interests

  • Robust signal processing and high-dimensional detection — inference under heavy-tailed noise, applied to activity detection in massive random access.
  • Efficient fine-tuning of large language models — low-rank and CP tensor adapters; zeroth-order Riemannian optimization on fixed-rank manifolds.
  • Structured signal models and inverse problems — graph signal processing, GNN sensitivity, and tensor decompositions for hyperspectral imaging.
