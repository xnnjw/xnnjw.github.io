Hello!

I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering in 2026 at Aalto University, advised by Professors Esa Ollila and Sergiy A. Vorobyov. I received my Master degree in Acoustics from Aalto University in 2022. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree in Information Engineering in 2020 at Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China.

My research lies at the intersection of signal processing, optimization, and machine learning, with a focus on robust inference and structured models.
