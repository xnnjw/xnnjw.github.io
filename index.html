Hello! I am a third year Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering at Aalto University, advised by Prof. Esa Ollila and Prof. Sergiy A. Vorobyov.

I obtained my Master degree in Acoustics from Aalto University in 2022. Prior to that, I did my Bachelor degree in Information Engineering in Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2020.

My research lies at the intersection of signal processing, optimization, and machine learning, with a focus on robust inference and structured models.

Research Interests

Robust signal processing and statistical inference, with applications to high-dimensional and structured data.

Graph signal processing, including GNN sensitivity analysis under model uncertainty.

Tensor methods and structured regression models, particularly Kruskal tensor regression under constraints.
